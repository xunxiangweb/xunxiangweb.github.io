Xunxiang，


Accompanying international students to find their future directions.



Xunxiang is a non-profit organization initiated by North American international students in 2018; its purpose is to help international students understand more career directions and future development possibilities through career interviews, mentorship program, learning and development events, etc., connecting students and professionals in the workplace and enabling the knowledge, experience, and resources to be passed on among international students community.

These years，we've done...

  • We interviewed 48 seniors from different industries, covering not only popular industries such as the Internet, consulting, and finance, but also niche areas such as social sciences, academic research, self-media bloggers, and overseas entrepreneurship.

  • Xunxiang has 15 large and small sharing communities, covering nearly 5000 international students, parents and professionals.

  • The trainee category included 44 students from North American universities, and 17 professionals were invited to join as Mentor, and a total of 120+ times of free public welfare one-on-one office hours were organized. Trainees have already obtained internships or full-time company offers including Microsoft, EY, PwC, Oracle, ByteDance.

  • Xunxiang has been established for 900+ days, and upholds the original intention of public welfare and the concept of mutual assistance to help international students find a better future.

Karen Zheng

就读于University of Pennsylvania
寻向练习生

寻向的大家都很暖，寻求帮助的时候大家都会竭尽所能。寻向的大家都很强，各行各业优秀的同学都在向着自己的目标和理想努力。寻向的大家都很会，所有话题都能遇到能侃侃而谈，迸发火花的人。从学长学姐那里获得帮助再进而用自己知道的帮助学弟学妹，我在寻向看到了无私传承。所以，寻向入股不亏！
人生的路总是孤独，但有志同道合的人，不论怎样都会精彩。来到寻向后，我意识到我们并不孤单，也不是一个人在战斗。在寻向，努力认真的学弟学妹激励着我，同龄人的理想激励着我，寻向作为一个互帮互助的团体，不断给予我继续向上的动力。同样，在如此特殊的留学环境下，寻向的存在给予了这个团体中的每一个成员坚持的勇气、成功的信心，以及对美好未来的希望。作为一名领队，我很开心能跟寻向一起成长、一起进步。愿终有一天，寻向的名字可以和埋藏在大家心中的梦想一起，发光发热。
