What do we offer?

Mentorship Pairing

Students will be paired with a young professional and meet their mentors regularly to receive career development support and guidance.

1-on-1 Office Hour

Time designed for mentees to meet any of the 40+ professionals to form their understanding of a variety of industries and career trajectories.

Learning Pathway

Our learning pathway covers comprehensive career-related knowledge and skills which lays a strong foundation for students' success in recruiting.

Peer Sharing

A decentralized community in which everyone shares and benefits from others' sharing.
